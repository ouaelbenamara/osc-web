OSC Community
OPEN SOURCE CLUB EXPANDING KNOWLEDGE, SHAPING FUTURES.

WEBELIEVE

“ At Open Source Club, we believe that open-source represents the future of technology: a world where ideas, knowledge, and code are shared freely and innovated upon without boundaries. Collaboration and transparency drive the evolution of tech, and open-source empowers everyone to make a difference. By embracing open-source, we build a community where creativity and problem-solving know no limits, helping to shape a more connected and equitable future for all. ”

Our Main Goal as in our slogan is to expand knowledge and shape futures Insha-allah, for that we organize events, build projects, create content, develop community, provide courses, share our own experiences, grow people newtorks and create connections with amazing people !
